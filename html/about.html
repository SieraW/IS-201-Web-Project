Meet The Creators

Karl & Siera White

Origin Story

Karl and Siera live in the quiet woods of Helsinki, Finland with their pet flying squirrel, Robert. They met on an excursion through the Amazon rainforest searching for rare, endangered song-birds. Siera likes to tell people that she got to take one home with her, but Karl just rolls his eyes.

Karl started singing at the age of 4 and has been performing in choral works for over 20 years. Siera is an assassin behind the keyboard making web designs. This unlikely pairing of hunter and hunted are now married and happy owners of the audio-recording studio White Noise. They are applauded for recording famous tracks such as, “Where ma fon go?” and “Dreams of a Dusty Bottlecap”. They expect to be seen at the Emmy awards in 2023.
